Hibiscus is high in antioxidants and offers many potential benefits. In particular, it may help promote weight loss, reduce the growth of bacteria and cancer cells, and support the health of the heart and liver. Hibiscus is available as an extract or, more often, a tea.You can find hibiscus tea bags at some grocery stores, specialty tea shops or online. You can also make hibiscus tea by covering 1 tablespoon of dried hibiscus with 8 ounces of boiled water and letting it steep for around 10 minutes. Once steeped, pour the tea through a fine-mesh sieve to remove the solids. The exact steeping time will depend on how strong you like your tea.
